9/11 Memorial Stair Climb

The 11th Annual 9/11 Stair Climb will be held on September 11, 2023 at Donald W. Reynolds Razorback Stadium.

The Gates will open at 5:30 AM and the Climb will start at 5:45 AM.

Free parking will be avaliable at Lot 44 from 5 AM to 8 AM. Stadium entry will be through gate 14 near the Razorback fountain.

You can register yourself or your organization by clicking on "RSVP" below.

If you require additional information, please contact us using the button below.

We would like to thank the University of Arkansas Police Department for their help in organizing this event.
